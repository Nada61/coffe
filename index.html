About Us

Since 2001, we’ve been brewing our way across Egypt delivering the best in coffee, food and community. Having introduced the contemporary coffee bar culture to Egypt, our strategic growth and defined objectives have secured our position as one of the key coffee brands in the market. With 27 branches throughout Cairo and Alexandria and an impressive schedule for further expansion, Beano’s Cafe is the place to be! Step inside any one of our locations and you’ll see what we mean. Our cozy and inviting interiors are made for all good things—good coffee, good food and good company.
